# Chocolate Sales Analysis
**Datasource**: Chocolate Sales Data from Kaggle's  (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/atharvasoundankar/chocolate-sales).

### Objective
Analyze sales data to discover international chocolate market customer behavior. Identify bestsalers products and the correlation between price and volume sold.

### What was done?
A market analysis was conducted using open data on chocolate international sales. The analysis included data preprocessing, exploratory analysis, and visualization to identify trends, identify bestsalers products and the correlation between price and volume sold, and extract insights relevant to business decision-making.
